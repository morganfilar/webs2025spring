Packing Tips!

1. Roll, Don’t Fold

Rolling your clothes saves space and reduces wrinkles. It’s perfect for squeezing more outfits into your carry-on without making it a mess!

2. Use Packing Cubes

Packing cubes help keep your suitcase organized and make unpacking a breeze. You can separate clothes by type, outfits, or days of travel.

3. Always Bring a Portable Charger

Whether you're snapping pics or navigating new cities, your phone is essential. A backup charger is a lifesaver when you're on the go all day.

4. Pack a Mini First Aid Kit

A few bandaids, pain relievers, and allergy meds can save your day. You never know when you’ll need a quick fix on the road.

5. Leave Room for Souvenirs

Don’t overpack—trust me, you’ll want to bring back goodies! Leave a little extra space (or pack a foldable tote) for your finds.
